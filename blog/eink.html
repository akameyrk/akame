What's E-Ink?

E-Ink devices simulate the 'paper-feel' of books through a screen. They offer longer battery life, lower refresh rates, and are easier on the eyes.

Kindle: My first E-Ink device, likely the best starter e-reader on the market. It only reads books.

Boox Go: An E-Ink tablet that can do everything the Kindle can, plus additional features like note-taking and sketching.

Boox Go Review

I've now had the Boox Go for a few months and it's been great. I've never had a device that you can write on so it's kinda epic. I use it for sketching (which i've realized I enjoy a lot more than I thought), notetaking, journaling and reading.

I don't think this device is for everyone- it's quite expensive and you could probably find cheaper alternatives that do similar tasks. Though, if you enjoy a big screen, are an e-ink enthusiast, and a pen (fancy), then go for it.
