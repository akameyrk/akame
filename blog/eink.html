what's eink

devices that try to simulate that 'paper-feel' that books have, but through a screen. the battery life is a lot higher, have low refresh rates, and are easy on the eyes.

kindle: my first eink device, probably the best starter e-reader on the market. only reads books

boox go: eink tablet that can do everything the kindle can, but also a bunch of other stuff like notetaking & sketching.
